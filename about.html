About Jenny

Educator & Coach

Teaching and coaching are at the heart of what I do. I've conducted hundreds of trainings and seminars, on topics from business building to leadership development, and served as an adjunct professor of Real Estate Law at Midlands Technical College. As a Newfield Certified Coach, John Maxwell Certified Trainer, and a Certified Taking Flight with DISC Facilitator, I bring structured, actionable frameworks to help individuals and teams unlock their potential.

Business Development

I am a lawyer by training, with a Juris Doctor from Campbell University. After practicing commercial real estate, I spent nearly 20 years working inside a Fortune 500 company where I rose to senior executive leadership. During that time, I managed the company's top clients, contributed to numerous committees and played a key role in shaping strategic planning initiatives that guided the company's growth and transformation. This experience gave me a deep understanding of how large organizations operate — from governance and compliance to culture and customer experience.

For my corporate clients, this background is invaluable. I know firsthand the challenges of aligning diverse stakeholders, navigating complex regulatory environments, and driving results at scale. My ability to combine legal expertise, executive leadership, and strategic insight allows me to help organizations not only set ambitious goals but also build the systems, teams, and culture to achieve them.

Beyond Business

Community and personal wellness have always been central to my life. I am passionate about fitness and health, and I've been a runner since the 7th grade. Over the years, I've completed three full marathons, 1 Olympic Triathlon and dozens of half marathons, most recently crossing the finish line at the Chicago Bank of America Marathon in October 2025.
